🚖 NYC Taxi Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard explores over 10 million taxi trips in NYC during January 2015, focusing on trip duration, pickup/dropoff trends, weather impacts, and flight correlations. The goal is to make data-driven decisions easy through clear storytelling.

📺 Demo Walkthrough

📊 Key Insights

  • Peak Demand Hours: Most taxi pickups occur between 5 PM and 8 PM.
  • Weather Impact: Rainy days saw a 20% increase in taxi usage.
  • Airport Demand: JFK and LaGuardia locations consistently have high drop-off rates.
  • Day-wise Analysis: Saturdays show the longest average trip durations.
